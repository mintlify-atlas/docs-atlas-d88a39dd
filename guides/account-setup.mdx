Ave uses passkeys instead of passwords to keep your account secure. During registration, you'll create your identity, set up a passkey, and configure recovery methods.

## Creating Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start Registration">
    Navigate to the registration page and begin creating your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Your Identity">
    Fill in your profile information:
    
    - **Handle**: A unique username (3-32 characters, letters, numbers, and underscores only)
    - **Display Name**: Your full name or preferred display name
    - **Email**: Optional, for notifications and recovery
    - **Birthday**: Optional
    - **Avatar**: Optional profile picture
    
    Click **Continue** when ready.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Up Your Passkey">
    Click **Set up passkey** to create your passwordless authentication method.
    
    Your browser will prompt you to:
    - Use Touch ID, Face ID, or Windows Hello (biometric authentication)
    - Or use your device PIN as an alternative
    
    <Note>
      Your passkey is stored securely on your device and cannot be accessed by Ave or anyone else. It uses WebAuthn technology built into modern browsers and operating systems.
    </Note>
    
    When you see the authentication prompt, verify your identity using your preferred method. Ave will generate a master encryption key in your browser that never leaves your device.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Up Recovery Options">
    To protect your account if you lose access to your passkey, Ave provides recovery methods.
    
    ### Trust Codes
    
    You'll receive 2 trust codes in the format: `XXXXX-XXXXX-XXXXX-XXXXX-XXXXX`
    
    <Warning>
      Save these trust codes in a secure location immediately. You'll need them if you lose access to your passkey.
      
      - Store them in a password manager
      - Write them down and keep them in a safe place
      - Never share them with anyone
    </Warning>
    
    Check the box confirming you've saved your trust codes, then click **Continue**.
    
    Trust codes encrypt a backup of your master encryption key, allowing you to recover your account on a new device.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Registration">
    Your account is now ready! You'll be automatically logged in and can start using Ave.
    
    You'll see your Dashboard with access to:
    - Your profile and identity information
    - Device management
    - Security settings
    - Activity log
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Happens Behind the Scenes

Ave uses end-to-end encryption (E2EE) to protect your data:

1. **Master Key Generation**: A unique encryption key is generated in your browser using cryptographically secure random numbers
2. **Passkey Creation**: Your device creates a passkey using WebAuthn that's tied to your biometric or device PIN
3. **Trust Code Encryption**: Your master key is encrypted with your trust codes so you can recover it later
4. **Secure Storage**: Only encrypted data is sent to Ave's servers - your master key never leaves your device in plaintext

<Note>
  Ave cannot access your master encryption key or decrypt your data. Only you have the keys to your encrypted information.
</Note>

## Handle Requirements

Your handle is your unique identifier on Ave:

- Must be 3-32 characters long
- Can only contain letters (a-z, A-Z), numbers (0-9), and underscores (_)
- Must be unique across all Ave users
- Case-insensitive (saved as lowercase)
